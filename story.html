Oklohoma, Route 66 | Story Info

Simple Story Info

Journey: Families loaded their belongings onto vehicles and embarked on a journey filled with hope and uncertainty. The road was often unforgiving, and the search for work led them through makeshift camps and roadside settlements.

Living Conditions: Impoverished families faced challenges of makeshift shelters, limited resources, and the constant struggle for work. The journey was a poignant tale of resilience amid adversity.
